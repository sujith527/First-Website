Roronoa Zoro - The Legendary Swordsman

Who is Roronoa Zoro?

Zoro, the right-hand man of Monkey D. Luffy, is a swordsman whose only goal is to surpass Dracule Mihawk and become the greatest swordsman in the world.

Power & Abilities

Mastering the Santoryu (Three-Sword Style), Zoro wields legendary swords like Wado Ichimonji, Sandai Kitetsu, and Enma, slicing through steel and even mountains.

Iconic Moments

"Nothing happened." - Zoro endured unimaginable pain to protect his captain, proving his undying loyalty.
Zoro vs King - One of his most epic battles, showcasing his raw power and unmatched sword skills.

Famous Quote

"Scars on the back are a swordsman's shame."
